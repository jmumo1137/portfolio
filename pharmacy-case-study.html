Pharmacy POS & Inventory System

End-to-end point-of-sale and inventory management system designed for pharmacy operations. Covers product management, sales, stock control, reporting, and role-based access.

  • Role: Full-Stack Developer
  • Type: Business Operations System
  • Stack: Node.js, Express, SQL, React, Role-Based Access

Problem Statement

Manual pharmacy management leads to stock discrepancies, slow sales processing, inaccurate reporting, and difficulties in managing multiple roles across staff.

The challenge was to create a reliable, end-to-end system that integrates sales, inventory, and reporting while enforcing role-based access and data integrity.

Solution Overview

The Pharmacy POS system centralizes all operations into a single platform:

  • Real-time inventory management and stock alerts
  • Role-based access for cashier, manager, and admin
  • Sales transactions and invoice generation
  • Reporting dashboards for performance and stock analysis

System Architecture

Key Features

  • Product and category management
  • Stock control with alerts for low inventory
  • POS workflow for cashier and manager
  • Sales and invoice tracking
  • Role-based authentication and authorization

Security & Reliability

  • JWT-based authentication
  • Role-based access control (Admin, Manager, Cashier)
  • Server-side validations to prevent stock or sales inconsistencies
  • Error handling and logging for all critical operations

Results & Impact

  • Improved stock accuracy and traceability
  • Faster sales processing and invoicing
  • Centralized reporting for managers and admins
  • Reduced human errors and operational friction
